# T4001 Employers' Guide - Payroll Deductions and Remittances

Official source: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/publications/t4001.html

CRA guide version observed: 2025 online/PDF guide available as t4001-25e.pdf. Page details observed: 2026-01-21.

## Scope

T4001 is the CRA payroll guide for employers, trustees, and payers who need information about deducting and remitting:

- Canada Pension Plan contributions.
- Employment Insurance premiums.
- Income tax from amounts paid.

The T4001 guide should be used with current CRA payroll deduction tables, formulas, or the Payroll Deductions Online Calculator when calculating source deductions.

## Advisor usage

Use T4001 for employer payroll obligations, payroll account setup, deductions, remittance timing, reporting, and employer compliance questions. Do not use it to calculate payroll deductions without checking current CRA payroll tables/formulas for the pay period and province.

For taxable benefits and allowances, use T4130. For general corporation tax use T4012. For GST/HST use RC4022.
