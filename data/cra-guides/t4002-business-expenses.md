# T4002 Self-employed Business, Professional, Commission, Farming, and Fishing Income

Official source: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/publications/t4002.html

CRA guide version observed: 2025 online/PDF guide available as t4002-25e.pdf. Page details observed: 2026-04-10.

## Scope

T4002 is primarily for self-employed business, professional, commission, farming, and fishing income. For a corporation, it is not the T2 filing guide, but it is useful general CRA context for business income and expense concepts.

The guide discusses business expense classification, including:

- Current expenses: recurring expenses or costs that restore property to original condition.
- Capital expenses: costs that provide lasting benefit, improve property beyond original condition, represent a separate asset, or are significant relative to the property.
- Capital cost allowance context for capital property.

## Advisor usage

Use T4002 cautiously for general business expense reasoning, especially current vs capital expense questions. For corporation-specific tax filing, use T4012. For GST input tax credits, use RC4022. For fixed assets in this app, consider whether the cost should be expensed, capitalized, or reviewed by a CPA.
