# T4012 T2 Corporation Income Tax Guide

Official source: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/publications/t4012/t2-corporation-income-tax-guide.html

CRA guide version observed: T4012(E) Rev. 24, "T2 Corporation - Income Tax Guide - 2024". Date modified: 2024-04-18.

CRA caution: This guide uses plain language to explain the most common tax situations. It is provided for information only and does not replace the law. Unless otherwise stated, legislative references are to the Income Tax Act or Income Tax Regulations.

## Scope

The T4012 guide explains common corporation income tax situations for corporations filing a T2 Corporation Income Tax Return. It is organized by pages and chapters of the T2 return.

Topics include:

- Page 1 of the T2 return: corporation identification and filing information.
- Page 2 of the T2 return: attachments, information schedules and forms, and calculation schedules.
- Pages 3 to 5 of the T2 return: taxable income and tax calculation areas.
- Pages 6 and 7 of the T2 return: refundable portion of Part I tax, refundable dividend tax on hand, and dividend refund.
- Page 8 and page 9 of the T2 return: additional tax return areas and related corporation schedules.
- Appendices: federal, provincial, and territorial corporation schedules and forms; handling business taxes online; electronic payments.

## Advisor usage

For an Alberta corporation, use this guide for federal corporation return context and T2 filing questions. Do not use it as a substitute for tax law, a completed T2 return, or CPA review. For GST/HST questions, use RC4022. For payroll and employee benefits, use T4001 and T4130.
