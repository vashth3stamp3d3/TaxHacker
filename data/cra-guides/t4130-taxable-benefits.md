# T4130 Employers' Guide - Taxable Benefits and Allowances

Official source: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/publications/t4130/employers-guide-taxable-benefits-allowances.html

CRA guide version observed: T4130 Rev. 25.

## Scope

T4130 is the CRA guide for employers that provide employees with benefits or allowances. It helps determine whether a benefit is taxable and how to report it.

Topics include:

- Automobiles and motor vehicles.
- Board and lodging.
- Gifts and awards.
- Group term life insurance.
- Interest-free or low-interest loans.
- Meals.
- Security options.
- Tool reimbursements and allowances.
- Transit passes.
- Tuition fees.

## Advisor usage

Use T4130 when a transaction or payroll item may be a taxable benefit, allowance, reimbursement, or employee-related cost. Consider whether the benefit is taxable, whether CPP/EI/income tax deductions apply, and how it should be reported on slips. Use T4001 for deduction/remittance mechanics.
